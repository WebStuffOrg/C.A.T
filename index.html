About the Exhibition

Cats have fascinated and accompanied humanity for thousands of years, crossing geographical, cultural and spiritual boundaries. Originally considered sacred and revered creatures, cats have played different roles in various societies: from symbols of luck and protection to protagonists of myths and superstitions. With the expansion of civilizations, cats have spread to every corner of the globe, becoming not only beloved pets, but also subjects of art, literature and popular culture. Our journey was born out of curiosity to explore not only the history of the cat as a pet, but also how it has influenced human culture.
It is this extraordinary bond between man and cat that has led us to create an exhibition that explores the cultural impact of these fascinating felines. We hope this exhibition offers a new perspective on the importance and timeless charm of cats, leading visitors to discover the deep connection that unites them with us.
